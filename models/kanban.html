Kanban: advantages and disadvantages

Advantages Disadvantages
♚ Kanban is a very simple and easy to understand system. ♿ Kanban cannot be used as an independant tool.
♚Better visibility. Using the Kanban board, everyone can instantly see how tasks are moving through the process. ♿An outdated Kanban board that can lead to issues in the development
♚Improved flow efficiency. Visualizing your process will highlight areas of inefficiency very quickly. ♿The board can be overcomplicated.
♚Increased productivity Kanban benefits your productivity by shifting the focus from starting work to finishing work. ♿Lack of timing. There is no timeframes associated with each phase.
